ABOUT

Kal

Personal Trainer

Kal's athletic journey has started from a very young age of 7 learning Shotokan Karate. Fast forward decades later, he was unable to shake the martial arts itch and found the art of Muay Thai. After competing for several years on the amateur circuit he decided to turn his attention to personal training. Pulling from his experience and knowledge from training and competing, Kal focuses on strength and conditioning as well as mobility. From bodyweight to barbell exercises he is able to increase your strength and cardiovascular endurance all while having fun and at the end of the day having you feel great about yourself!
